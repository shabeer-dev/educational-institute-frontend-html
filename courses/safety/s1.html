FIRE& SAFETY ENGINEERING

Fire and safety engineering is a field that focuses on preventing and managing fire-related incidents in various settings, including residential, commercial, and industrial environments. The main objective of fire and safety engineering is to protect people, property, and the environment from the destructive effects of fire. Some of the key areas of focus in fire and safety engineering include:

  • Fire detection and prevention systems - This includes the design, installation, and maintenance of fire alarms, sprinklers, and other fire suppression systems.
  • Building codes and regulations - Fire and safety engineers must be familiar with building codes and regulations to ensure that buildings are constructed and maintained in compliance with safety standards.
  • Emergency planning and response - Fire and safety engineers must develop emergency plans and procedures to ensure that people can evacuate safely in the event of a fire, and that emergency responders can effectively manage the situation.
