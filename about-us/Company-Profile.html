Company Profile

Gasline Quality & Inspection Service (GQIS)is established in 2010, is a continuing technical education institute, which had been accredited by American Society for Non-Destructive testing as Oil& Gas training institute, Scientific & technical education development council & WHRDE. GQIS is a facility dedicated to providing academic instruction and resources to students at various levels of education. The primary goal of GQIS institute is to impart knowledge, skills, and values that prepare students for their future academic and professional pursuits. We employ trained and experienced faculty members, who develop and deliver curricula that cover a broad range of subjects and disciplines. In addition to classroom instruction, educational institutes may also offer extracurricular activities, sports programs, and student organizations to help students develop socially and holistically. Overall, educational institutes play a critical role in shaping the intellectual and personal development of students and preparing them to become responsible and successful members of society.

Since 2010 we are providing Engineering Education and gasline stands top in producing quality and industry ready graduates. The state-of-the-art Infrastructure creates practical knowledge and generates innovation and creativity. Sculpt your Career with gasline quality and inspection service.
